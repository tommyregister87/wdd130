Site Purpose & Goals:

The purpose of this site is to open up an online store for my grandfather art to be sold.
There will be catagories depending on the scenery type. With this, I will discuss his
experience, display his work, and eventually help post tutorial videos of how to create
the pictures yourself. Overall, this site will be used as a cost effective way for consumers
to not only purchase fine art themselves, but to also grow their own skillsets.

Some of the things needed for the site are About Me, Gallary, and Contacts pages.
Moreover, the Gallary will have prices attacked to each individual picture.
One things that I would like to attempt on the home page is a horizontal picture
scroll to keep the page simplistic. I am going to attempt the "less is more" approch
to this website by utilizing lines rather than boxes.

Target Audience:

Those looking to buy art, and eventually to help potential atrists themselves.
